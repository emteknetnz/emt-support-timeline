Status definitions

Full support Receives bugfixes and security fixes for all vulnerabilities
Partial support Receives security fixes for high or critical vulnerabilities
Unsupported No longer supported and receives no updates
Pre-release Available for testing but not yet officially released
In development Currently under active development
Planned Scheduled for future development and release

Support length definitions

Standard 6 months of full support followed by 6 months of partial support
Extended 12 months of full support followed by 12 months of partial support
